Raiden from 'Metal Gear Rising: Revengeance'

A game developed by PlatinumGames

Raiden, real name Jack, is the main protagonist of Metal Gear Solid 2: Sons of Liberty and Metal Gear Rising: Revengeance games and the secondary protagonist of Metal Gear Solid 4: Guns of the Patriots.

He is a Liberian-American mercenary and former special forces soldier.
